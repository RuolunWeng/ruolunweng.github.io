About ME - CV

Allen Weng
RIM[Realisateur en Informmatique Musicale] and Sound engineer
 I was born in 1991 in china, I studied music technology in Shanghai Conservatory of Music from 2010 to 2014, and I continues my master in music technology. From september 2016, I started my MASTER II RIM in University Jean Monnet in Saint Etienne, France.
I always participates in live musical production: real-time interactive music, sound and visual installation. Since 2010, I has participated in EMW 2011(Electronic Music Week in Shanghai), ICMC 2012, NIME 2012, EMW 2013,EMW 2015, “Rising Artist Work”in Shanghai International Festival of Art. I also keeps close cooperation in research, master classes, and realization of concert with GRAME, CIRM, and CCRMA.
